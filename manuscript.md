## SECTION 2.4 — TECHNICAL ARCHITECTURE

**Headline:**
## Enterprise-Grade Architecture Under the Hood

**Body:**
TaveStack OS is built on a cloud-native microservices foundation, designed for security, scale, and speed at every layer.

**Architecture Highlights:**
- **Multi-tenant with data isolation** — Every organization's data is logically separated at the kernel level. No shared tables, no leakage.
- **Event-driven backbone** — All modules communicate through a real-time event bus. Actions in one module instantly propagate across the OS.
- **API-first design** — Every module, every feature, every TAVE interaction is accessible via our REST and GraphQL APIs. Build on top of TaveStack. Extend anything.
- **Edge-deployed** — TaveStack OS is deployed across global edge nodes, ensuring sub-200ms response times for users from Lagos to London.
- **TAVE runtime isolation** — Each organization's TAVE instance runs in an isolated AI execution environment. Your TAVE learns only from your data. Never shared.
- **End-to-end encryption** — Data at rest (AES-256) and in transit (TLS 1.3). Encryption keys per organization.

## SECTION 7.4 — FAQ

**Q: Is the OS really free forever?**
Yes. The TaveStack OS — your workspace, user management, TAVE (limited), and core navigation — will always be free. We monetize apps, not access.

**Q: Can I activate and deactivate app modules?**
Absolutely. You're billed monthly (or annually if you chose that). Deactivate any app at the end of your billing period with no penalty. Your data is retained for 90 days.

**Q: What happens to my data if I cancel?**
We give you a 90-day window to export all your data in standard formats (CSV, JSON, PDF). After 90 days, data is permanently deleted.

**Q: How is TAVE billed?**
TAVE is included in the OS. On the Free plan, you get 50 TAVE commands/month. On Grow and Enterprise, TAVE commands are unlimited.

**Q: Do you offer local currency billing?**
Yes. We support billing in NGN, KES, GHS, ZAR, USD, EUR, and GBP. Contact us for other currencies.

**Q: Is there a free trial of paid modules?**
Each paid module comes with a 14-day free trial. No credit card required to start.

**Q: Do you offer NGO or government pricing?**
Yes. We offer up to 40% discount for registered NGOs and government institutions. Contact sales@tavestack.com.

# APPENDIX A: GLOBAL TONE & VOICE GUIDE

**Voice:** Authoritative, clear, and bold. TaveStack speaks like a founder who knows exactly what problem they've solved — not like a committee that approved a press release.

**Tone Principles:**
- **Confident, not arrogant** — State what TaveStack does without hedging. "TAVE executes" not "TAVE may help you execute."
- **Simple, not simplistic** — Explain complex architecture in plain language. Never dumb it down. Trust the reader.
- **Specific, not vague** — "68% productivity gain in 90 days" beats "significant improvement."
- **Human, not corporate** — Use contractions. Use "you" and "your." Write as if talking to a smart operator across a table.
- **African pride, global ambition** — Never apologize for where we're from. Africa is a feature, not a footnote.

**Words to Avoid:**
- "Synergy" / "Leverage" / "Ecosystem" (unless technical)
- "World-class" (show it, don't say it)
- "Revolutionary" (earned by the product)
- "We are excited to announce" (be direct)
- "Solution" as a standalone noun

**Words to Use:**
- "OS" / "Operating System" (always)
- "Executes" (for TAVE's actions)
- "Deploy" (for activating modules)
- "Native" (for integrations and TAVE access)
- "Intelligent" (not "smart")
- "Transparent pricing"
